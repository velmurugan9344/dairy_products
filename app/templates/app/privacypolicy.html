Privacy Policy

Last updated: july 29,2024



Introduction to Privacy Policy :

This privacy policy (the "Privacy Policy") applies to your use of the website of Oscar Dairy Products hosted at oscardsairyproducts.com. It outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information. By using our website, you agree to the terms of this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our website. This policy does not apply to third-party websites linked from our site.

Information We Collect and How We Use It :

We collect personal information such as your name, email address, and phone number when you register. This information helps us provide our services, process orders, and improve customer experience. We may also collect payment information for transaction purposes. We use this data to inform you about updates and offers. Your information is stored securely and only used for its intended purpose.

Account Information :

When you create an account, we collect personal details like your name, email, and contact number. This information allows you to access our full range of services. We may also store sensitive data such as payment details securely. You will receive updates and newsletters based on your preferences. You can modify your information in your account settings.

Customer Information :

We store information such as your address, mobile number, and payment details when you make a purchase. This data is used to fulfill orders and provide customer support. We may share this information with trusted partners for transaction processing. Your data is kept confidential and secure. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties.

Activity :

We record information about your interactions with our website, such as pages viewed and search queries. This helps us analyze user behavior and improve our services. We may also collect technical information like IP addresses and browser types. This data is used to enhance security and optimize user experience. It is aggregated and anonymized for analysis.

Cookies :

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences. They allow us to customize your experience and gather usage statistics. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect website functionality. We do not use cookies to collect personal information.

Data Security :

Your account is protected by a password that you set. We implement industry-standard security measures to safeguard your personal information. Access to your data is restricted to authorized personnel only. We regularly review our security practices to ensure data protection. In case of any security breaches, we will notify you promptly.

Transfer of Information :

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except for trusted partners who assist in providing our services. These partners are obligated to maintain the confidentiality and security of your data. We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights. Your information may be transferred in case of a business merger or acquisition. We will notify you of any significant changes to data handling practices.

Links to Other Sites :

Our website may contain links to third-party sites for your convenience. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information. These links do not imply our endorsement of the linked sites. We do not share your personal information with these third parties.

User Access to Personal Information :

As a registered user, you can access and update your personal information through your account settings. You have the right to request the deletion of your data at any time. We will respond to such requests promptly and ensure your information is securely removed. You can also update your privacy preferences from your account. Contact us if you need assistance with data management.

Changes to this Privacy Policy :

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices. Any modifications will be posted on this page with the updated date. We encourage you to review this policy regularly to stay informed. Your continued use of our website indicates acceptance of the changes. If you disagree with any updates, please discontinue use of our services.

Contact Us :

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@murugangv555.com. We are committed to protecting your privacy and will address any concerns promptly. Your feedback helps us improve our privacy practices. You can also reach us at our mailing address provided on the website. Thank you for trusting Oscar Dairy Products.
